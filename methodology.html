Methodology

Considering the purpose of the final objective, there is no clearly established methodology to be followed so as to directly dive into the design of the framework. For that reason, a design methodology is established and steps that are taken to develop the final objective are defined.

01

Main Project Themes

Regarding the theme of renovation, the primary purpose of this projects is to examine the renovation of roofs on residential houses. The renovation process involves all activities from initiation of the project up to the finalization of the roof renovation of roof and cleaning up the site.
The second subject matter is linked to the notion of industrialization and digitalization and explores the innovative technologies which are emerging in the construction industry, and are transforming how products are being designed and produced.

02

Cross-Domain Knowledge

Investigation of knowledge from other domains aims to provide the answer to the first question mentioned in the problem statement:
Which methodologies and tools can be used for structuring domain knowledge?
Cross-domain knowledge encompasses the topics of knowledge acquisition, systems thinking, and data visualization.

03

Domain Knowledge

Exploration of the realm of domain knowledge focuses on answering the questions:
How is the process of renovating roofs currently executed in practice?
For the renovation process, the particulars regarding the process of roof replacement, as well as the process of mounting new elements to the roof, such as PV panels for example, are evaluated.
Which new A&R technologies can be implemented in the construction industry?
The introduction of the new transitional period in construction industry mentioned as Construction 4.0, construction automation, robot-oriented design or any other term, brought about by a myriad of various A&R technologies that could be considered for deployment in construction.

04

Structuring Domain Knowledge

Cross-domain knowledge provides tools for the task of structuring domain knowledge. Structured representations of the phases in renovation process, as well as A&R technologies are the final result of this step in the development.

05

Final Objective

Development and design of the framework bestows the evaluation of the final question from the problem statement: How is the renovation process influencing the deployment of A&R technologies, and vice versa, and is there value in simultaneously analyzing the process of renovation and deployment of A&R technologies?

Methodology

A design methodology is established to develop the final objective.

01

Main Project Themes

Regarding the theme of renovation, the primary purpose of this projects is to examine the renovation of roofs on residential houses. The renovation process involves all activities from initiation of the project up to the finalization of the roof renovation of roof and cleaning up the site.
The second subject matter is linked to the notion of industrialization and digitalization and explores the innovative technologies which are emerging in the construction industry, and are transforming how products are being designed and produced.

02

Cross-Domain Knowledge

Investigation of knowledge from other domains aims to provide the answer to which methodologies and tools can be used for structuring domain knowledge.
Cross-domain knowledge encompasses the topics of knowledge acquisition, systems thinking, and data visualization.

03

Domain Knowledge

Exploration of the realm of domain knowledge focuses on describing how the process of renovating roofs is currently executed in practice. There are various types of roof renovation, expanding from only replacing roof tiles, to insulating the roof, to replacing the complete roof by installing modular roof elements to the existing structure, or to mounting additional elements on the roof, such as PV panels. The different intervention measures in the roof renovations are explored to provide the definition of the renovation process in this project.
The introduction of Industry 4.0 to construction industry brought about by a myriad of various A&R technologies that could be considered for deployment in construction. Certain technologies are developed in other industries, some are developed specifically for construction, and they vary in technology readiness level, as well as feasibility for direct implementation in construction

04

Structuring Domain Knowledge

Cross-domain knowledge provides tools for the task of structuring domain knowledge. Structured representations of the phases in renovation process, as well as A&R technologies are the final result of this step in the development.

05

Final Objective

The designed framework addresses the connection between the domains of renovation process and A&R technologies that can be deployed in it. The effectiveness of analyzing the two fields within the domain knowledge in conjunction, rather than separate, is where the fundamental merit of this project resides. The interconnectedness of two fields shows that making changes in either of the fields requires the revision of the other one.
